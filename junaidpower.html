What We Do

Power Back-Up

The need for power back-up solutions is paramount to any serious organization in Nigeria today. Power back-up solutions as the name sounds is an uninterruptible power supply system that protects electrical appliances & IT infrastructure from power outage, power fluctuations and power surge amongst others. JUNAID Power has a dedicated engineering and technical team that implements and supports our aforementioned solutions. In addition, JUNAID Power is in partnership with renowned solar companies in China, India and UAE on; resource sourcing and technical partnership in implementing turnkey solar projects. The business outlets we have for our Power Back-Up Solutions are in Lagos, Abuja, Kaduna and Kano (Nigeria).

Power Generation

The need for reliable and stable energy supply in Nigeria and Africa cannot be over emphasised. one One of the greatest challenge Nigeria as a nation faces today is the provision of uninterruptible power, hence the need for all stakeholders not just the Government to partner in ensuring a sustainable power supply in the country. We specialize in mostly Captive Power solutions by deploying Independent Power Plant (IPP) solution which is specially tailored for large scale commercial and industrial applications. Using Natural Gas as fuel, JUNAID Power covers the IPP CapEx and OpEx and provides non-interrupted Clean Energy solutions to large scale users with an aggregate demand of 1MW to 10MW, 24/7/365 non-stop on a agreed cost effective Tariff. Thus, saving the Users; Generator Capital Cost, Life Fueling Cost, Routine Services Cost, Life Replacement Costs, Grid Supply Costs which combine to huge energy costs for large scale consumers yearly, amongst others. We specifically cater to Users in these categories: Hospitality sector, Education Sector, Telecommunications Sector, Manufacturing Industries, Factories, Banking Sector, Aviation Sector, Housing Estates, Large commercial Shopping Malls & Tourist Centers, and the likes.
